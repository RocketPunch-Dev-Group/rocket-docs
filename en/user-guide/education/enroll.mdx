Once you've found a program that fits your goals on Rocketpunch, enrollment is straightforward. Depending on the provider, you'll either apply directly through Rocketpunch or be redirected to the training institution's own registration page to complete the process. Either way, the steps below walk you through exactly what to do.

## How to Enroll

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Education Section">
    Open [rocketpunch.com/en/edu](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/edu) and browse or search for a course. Use the keyword search, category filters, or the **Closing Soon** sort to surface relevant programs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on a Course">
    Select a program from the results list to open its detail page. Each listing shows the provider name, program format, schedule, and a summary of what you'll learn.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Full Details">
    Read the curriculum overview, session schedule, delivery format (online or offline), and any eligibility requirements carefully before proceeding. Confirm the application deadline so you're not caught out by a closing date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Enrollment or Registration Link">
    On the course detail page, click the enrollment or registration button. This either opens an application form hosted on Rocketpunch or takes you directly to the training provider's website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Enrollment with the Provider">
    Follow the provider's instructions to finish your application. This may include submitting personal information, uploading documents, or paying a course fee. Keep a confirmation email for your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Check Before Enrolling

Taking a few minutes to verify the following details prevents surprises after you've committed to a program.

**Eligibility requirements** — Some programs are restricted to employed workers, registered job seekers, recent graduates, or residents of a specific region. Confirm you qualify before applying.

**Online vs. offline format** — Online programs give you flexibility to study from anywhere, while offline programs require you to attend sessions at a fixed location. Hybrid programs combine both. Use the Online/Offline filter on the browse page to pre-screen for your preferred format.

**Application deadline** — Bootcamps and cohort-based academies have firm enrollment windows. Sort by **Closing Soon** on the education browse page to keep the most time-sensitive programs visible.

**Cost and funding options** — Many programs listed on Rocketpunch are eligible for HRD Korea (고용노동부 직업훈련) subsidies, which can significantly reduce or eliminate course fees for qualifying individuals. Look for HRD Korea badges or mentions in the course description, and check the K-Digital Training portal for subsidy eligibility details.

## For Training Providers

If you represent a bootcamp, academy, or online course platform and want to reach Rocketpunch's tech talent community, you can list your program for free at [enterprise.rocketpunch.com/edu/create](https://enterprise.rocketpunch.com/edu/create). See the [Enterprise guide](/en/user-guide/companies/enterprise) for more information on available tools for organisations.

<Note>
  Some courses redirect you to the training provider's own website to complete enrollment. If that happens, you're on the provider's platform — Rocketpunch is not involved in processing your application or payment from that point onward. Contact the provider directly if you have questions about your enrollment status.
</Note>
