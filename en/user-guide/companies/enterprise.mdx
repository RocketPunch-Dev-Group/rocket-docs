Rocketpunch Enterprise is the suite of tools designed for HR teams, recruiters, and organisations that want to do more than post a single job listing. Whether you're running a large-scale hiring campaign, promoting a developer event, listing a training course, or exploring advertising partnerships, Enterprise gives you a dedicated workspace to manage it all. Any company — from a two-person startup to a listed corporation — can access the core features for free at [enterprise.rocketpunch.com](https://enterprise.rocketpunch.com).

## Enterprise Features

**Job Postings with Quick Apply** — Publish open roles at no cost and let candidates apply instantly using their Rocketpunch profile. Manage all inbound applications from your Enterprise dashboard without switching tools. See [Post Jobs](/en/user-guide/companies/post-jobs) for a step-by-step guide.

**Event Creation and Attendee Recruitment** — Create a dedicated event page for tech meetups, recruiting events, hackathons, or online workshops. Attendees can register directly through Rocketpunch, and you can monitor sign-ups and send updates from your dashboard.

**Education and Training Course Listings** — Training providers, bootcamps, and academies can publish their programs to the Rocketpunch education section for free, reaching job seekers and professionals actively looking to upskill. See [Browse Courses](/en/user-guide/education/browse) for context on how candidates discover listings.

**Corporate Membership** — A corporate membership gives your company enhanced profile features including priority placement in company search results, a branded header, verified company badges, and additional analytics on profile views and job listing performance.

**Advertising Services** — Rocketpunch offers paid advertising placements across the platform — banner ads, featured job slots, and sponsored content — to amplify your employer brand and reach candidates who haven't discovered your company yet.

**Partnership Opportunities** — For co-marketing campaigns, content collaborations, or platform integrations, Rocketpunch's partnerships team is open to discussing custom arrangements suited to your organisation's goals.

## Getting Started

Visit [enterprise.rocketpunch.com](https://enterprise.rocketpunch.com) to create or log in to your company account. From the dashboard you can manage job listings, review applications, and access all the tools described above in one place.

<Note>
  For advertising inquiries or partnership opportunities, use the inquiry forms at the bottom of the events page at [rocketpunch.com/en/event](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/event). A member of the Rocketpunch team will follow up directly.
</Note>
