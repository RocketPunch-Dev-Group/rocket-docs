Rocketpunch Events is the tech community's hub for discovering and attending professional gatherings — from large-scale conferences to intimate networking meetups. Whether you're looking to expand your network, sharpen your skills, or explore a new industry, you can find and register for relevant events directly on the platform.

## Browsing Events

You have several ways to explore events on Rocketpunch. Visit [rocketpunch.com/en/event](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/event) to get started.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Browse by Type" icon="list">
    Filter events by format — conferences, lectures, networking events, or exclusive invite-only gatherings — to find the style of event that fits your goals.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Upcoming Events" icon="calendar-day">
    View a chronological list of events happening soon so you can plan ahead and register before spots fill up.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Browse by Topic" icon="tags">
    Explore events organized around specific industries or themes, such as AI, fintech, design, or entrepreneurship.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Calendar View" icon="calendar">
    Switch to the calendar view to see events laid out by date, making it easy to spot conflicts and plan your schedule.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Event Types

Rocketpunch supports a range of event formats to match different professional needs:

- **Conferences** — Large multi-session events featuring speakers, panels, and industry showcases.
- **Lectures** — Focused educational sessions led by experts on a specific topic or technology.
- **Networking Events** — Structured or informal gatherings designed to help professionals connect and build relationships.
- **Exclusive Events** — Invite-only or limited-access gatherings for select communities or company audiences.

## Joining an Event

Once you find an event that interests you, registering takes just a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Events page">
    Navigate to [rocketpunch.com/en/event](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/event). You'll see featured events and browsing options on the main page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your event">
    Use the **Browse by Type** or **Browse by Topic** filters to narrow your results, or switch to **Calendar View** to browse by date. You can also scroll the **Upcoming Events** list to see what's happening soon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View event details">
    Click on any event to open its detail page. Here you'll find the date, time, location (or virtual link), host organization, description, and any registration requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register or RSVP">
    Click the registration button on the event page to confirm your spot. Some events may redirect you to an external registration page hosted by the organizer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Hosting an Event

If you're an organizer or company looking to reach the Rocketpunch tech community, you can list your event on the platform at no cost.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create an Event" icon="circle-plus">
    List your conference, lecture, networking event, or exclusive gathering on Rocketpunch to reach thousands of tech professionals actively looking for events to attend.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Recruit Attendees" icon="user-plus">
    Promote your event to a targeted audience of developers, designers, founders, and other tech talent — and drive registrations directly through your event page.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

To create an event, visit [enterprise.rocketpunch.com/events/create](https://enterprise.rocketpunch.com/events/create) and complete the event submission form. You'll be able to set the event type, topic tags, date, location, and registration details.

Rocketpunch supports the following event types for organizers:

- Conferences
- Lectures
- Networking events
- Exclusive events

<Tip>
  Registering events on Rocketpunch is completely free for organizers. Listing your event here gives you direct access to one of Korea's most active tech talent communities.
</Tip>
