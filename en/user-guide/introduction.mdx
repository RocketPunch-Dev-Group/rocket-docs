Rocketpunch is Korea's leading professional network built for the tech community. Whether you're actively searching for your next role, exploring companies building exciting products, or growing your professional network, Rocketpunch gives you the tools to do it in one place. With over 170,000 indexed companies and a community of engineers, designers, PMs, and founders, the platform is where Korea's tech talent and the companies that hire them come together.

## Who Uses Rocketpunch

**Job seekers and professionals** use Rocketpunch to showcase their experience, find roles that match their stack, and stay connected with the broader tech community. **Companies and recruiters** use the platform to post positions, search for candidates, and build an employer brand through company profiles and events. **Developers and builders** can also integrate Rocketpunch data into their own products using the [developer Open API](/en/api/overview), accessing company and job information programmatically. Whatever your role in the ecosystem, Rocketpunch is designed to help you move faster and connect more meaningfully.

<Note>
  Rocketpunch is available in **11 languages**, with full support for both Korean and English. You can switch your display language at any time from your account settings. Most company profiles and job listings are published in Korean, but the platform interface is fully navigable in English.
</Note>
