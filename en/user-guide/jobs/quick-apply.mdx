Quick Apply is Rocketpunch's streamlined application method that lets you submit your application to a job posting without navigating away from the platform or filling out a separate form on a company's own website. Instead of re-entering your work history, skills, and contact details each time, Rocketpunch pulls the information directly from your profile and sends it to the hiring company on your behalf. The result is a faster, lower-friction path from discovering a role to getting your application in front of a recruiter.

## How Quick Apply Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a job with Quick Apply">
    Browse the [Jobs page](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/jobs) and look for listings that support Quick Apply. These postings let you complete the entire application without leaving Rocketpunch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the listing and click Apply">
    Open the job posting and click the **Apply** button. For Quick Apply listings, this initiates the in-platform application flow rather than redirecting you to an external site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your auto-populated details">
    Rocketpunch automatically fills in your application using your profile data — including your headline, work experience, education, skills, and contact information. Take a moment to confirm everything looks accurate and up to date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and submit">
    Once you're satisfied with the pre-filled information, confirm your application and submit. The process takes only a few seconds if your profile is current.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Application delivered">
    Your application is sent directly to the company through Rocketpunch. You don't need to manage a separate login or track a different system — everything stays within the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Companies Receive

When you Quick Apply to a listing, the hiring company receives your Rocketpunch profile as your application. This includes:

- **Profile headline and summary** — your self-described professional identity and background
- **Work experience** — roles, companies, durations, and descriptions you've added to your profile
- **Skills** — the technologies, tools, and competencies listed on your profile
- **Education** — academic background and certifications
- **Contact information** — the details you've made available for recruitment purposes

Because companies see your Rocketpunch profile directly, the completeness and quality of your profile has a direct impact on how your Quick Apply submissions are received. A sparse profile sends a weaker signal than a fully fleshed-out one — even if your underlying qualifications are strong.

<Tip>
  Keep your profile up to date so every Quick Apply submission reflects your latest experience, skills, and achievements. Updating your profile once benefits every future application automatically.
</Tip>

## Quick Apply vs. External Application

Companies on Rocketpunch choose how they accept applications. Here's how the two methods differ:

|  | Quick Apply | External Application |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Where you apply** | Directly on Rocketpunch | Company's own careers page or ATS |
| **Form to fill out** | None — your profile auto-populates | Varies by company |
| **What the company receives** | Your Rocketpunch profile | Whatever the company's system collects |
| **Speed** | Seconds | Depends on the external form |
| **Requires leaving Rocketpunch** | No | Yes |

<Note>
  Some companies offer both options on the same listing — a Quick Apply button alongside a link to their own career page. If you prefer to apply through a company's own system or want to include materials not on your Rocketpunch profile (such as a custom cover letter or portfolio), you can use the external link instead.
</Note>
