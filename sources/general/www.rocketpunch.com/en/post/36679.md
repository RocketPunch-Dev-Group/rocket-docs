They say that cloud and containers make our lives easier, but why do we find ourselves enslaved by infrastructure every day? 
 
Vanilla Kubernetes feels like a box full of parts, making it a pain to assemble and maintain it to fit the corporate environment. It’s time to step away from the exhausting management of assembling parts every time and learn to handle an 'enterprise-grade ultimate platform' that can manage large-scale clusters effortlessly. 
 
That platform is Red Hat OpenShift!

Cluster state awareness and monitoring: Perfect monitoring via web console and diagnosing CLI/API queries 
 
Real-world application deployment and storage: Pod-based container execution, troubleshooting, persistent data storage volume provisioning 
 
Ensuring reliability and lifecycle: High availability/resilience setup, safe updates, and flawless rollback
