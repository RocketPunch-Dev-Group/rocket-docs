Sharing information on the digital transformation of health examination data

📄 Are you still spending a lot of time managing health examination data manually? 
 
Every day, countless health examination documents are generated in hospitals. However, many medical institutions still manage data based on paper documents, resulting in significant time and manpower being used for search, storage, and data entry processes. 
 
Recently, SotaTek built a 'Healthcare OCR Solution' for a medical institution to convert paper-based health examination certificates into digital data and create an environment that can be integrated with hospital management systems. 
 
✔ Recognition of printed and handwritten data within health examination certificates 
✔ Automatic extraction and structuring of key information 
✔ Support for integration with HIS, EMR, and internal DB 
✔ OCR recognition accuracy of 95%+ 
✔ Reduction in manual input tasks and improvement in operational efficiency 
 
Through this project, the client was able to establish a unified system for storing, searching, and managing health examination data, leading to faster and more accurate work processes.
