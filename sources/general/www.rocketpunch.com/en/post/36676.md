AI Agents: The New Standard for Corporate Automation 
 
Companies have been introducing various automation systems such as ERP, electronic payment systems, and RPA for a long time to improve operational efficiency. However, as customer demands become more diverse and work environments more complex, it is becoming increasingly challenging to respond to the rapidly changing business environment with traditional rule-based automation alone. 
 
In this context of change, AI agents are gaining attention as a new alternative. 
 
While existing automation focuses on executing predefined procedures, AI agents understand the situation, analyze the necessary information, make judgments, and perform tasks independently. They differentiate from conventional automation by integrating multiple systems, utilizing unstructured data, and deriving optimal results tailored to business objectives. 
 
In particular, as many companies push for AI transformation (AX) beyond digital transformation (DX), AI agents have established themselves as key technologies across various industries, including customer service, manufacturing, logistics, and finance. 
 
AI projects do not end simply with the adoption of technology. Consideration must be given to integration with existing systems, continuous operation, and data management for them to lead to business outcomes. 
 
GITS serves as a technology partner supporting companies' AI transformations, from building AI agents to ERP integration, system operation, and maintenance, providing optimal solutions tailored to clients' business environments.
