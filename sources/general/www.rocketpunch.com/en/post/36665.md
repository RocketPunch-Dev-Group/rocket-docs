Network practical training course announcement

😱"Why does it work on my computer, but not on the server?" Escape the error hell with essential network skills! 
(CCNA Introduction) 
 
That moment every developer experiences, where there's no problem in the code, but an error like "Connection Refused" or "403 Forbidden" only occurs in the server environment. 
The real culprit behind this error, which even AI can't solve, is 'network'. 
 
Beyond simple coding, the ability to read the flow of data and understand where it is blocked in devices. As we transition into the era of infrastructure and cloud, the \[CCNA Network Administrator Introductory Course\] will start, helping senior developers and engineers build essential network skills! 
 
🖥️ Why 'Network' now? 
In an era where AI writes code for us, a developer's value is determined by their ability to trace and solve the root causes of failures. This is the most certain milestone for those looking to expand their careers as infrastructure engineers or cloud (AWS) experts, and for those who want to become indispensable aces in their companies. 
 
🛠 What will you learn? (Practical focus) 
This is a compressed practical course covering from small networks to enterprise environments, not mere theory memorization. 
 
Packet Analysis: Troubleshooting failures through OSI 7 Layer and TCP/IP analysis 
 
Network Design: Mastering IPv4 structure and subnetting (VLSM/CIDR) 
 
Practical Equipment: Cisco router/switch command and configuration in practice 
 
Operation/Security: Building VLAN, OSPF/EIGRP routing protocols, NAT, DHCP, Access-list 
 
💡 Learning Environment & Benefits 
Efficient weekday evenings: Intensive classes every Monday to Thursday from 19:30 to 22:30 (3 hours). Enjoy a complete break on weekends while maintaining a 'great life'! 
 
Hybrid Lecture: Option to choose between offline or online live classes at home 
 
Overwhelming cost-effectiveness: 90% government funding for small and medium-sized enterprises 
 
Final actual payment is 45,000 won (original price 450,000 won) 
 
Refund support for those who complete with 80% attendance or more (company prepayment/documentation required) 
 
📢 Recommended for: 
Junior developers who spend their time searching Google every time a server error occurs. 
 
Those considering a career transition into infrastructure, cloud, or network engineering. 
 
Job seekers looking to strengthen their CS and network fundamentals for a salary jump. 
 
In three weeks, your IT perspective will change completely. Check out the professional curriculum of Soldesk before the slots fill up!
