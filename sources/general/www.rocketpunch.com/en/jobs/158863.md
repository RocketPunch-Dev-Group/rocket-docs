Team Introduction

The Data Platform team designs and builds cloud-based service infrastructure, creating an environment where build, deployment, monitoring, and incident response are fast and stable. • Current focus areas: 1. Build GPU infrastructure and inference pipelines for the commercialization of LLM-based AI services 2. Enhance cloud infrastructure architecture in line with open platform expansion 3. Ensure stable processing systems for large traffic 4. Shorten deployment cycles for development organizations through CI/CD enhancements ※ This position will work under Curve Company upon joining based on the project operation structure, and is scheduled for employment succession to GC MediAI after March 2027.

Responsibilities

▶ Things you can do in this position • You will handle large-scale production traffic directly. You will experience Kafka-based streaming and Kubernetes cluster operations in an environment with over 200 million healthcare data transactions annually. • You will design cloud infrastructure architecture directly. With ongoing expansion into an open platform, you can collaboratively design and evolve the infrastructure structure to match service growth. • You will experience AI service infrastructure. You can directly build and operate the learning and inference pipelines for AI models in your own LLM and GPU cluster environment. You will also gain experience designing in environments that require data security and compliance due to the nature of the healthcare domain. • You will participate in technical decision-making. It is structured so that your proposals can be reflected in the selection of tools, pipeline design, and operational methods. ▶ Responsibilities 1. Design and build cloud-native platforms and infrastructures - Design, build, and operate Kubernetes-based container environments - Infrastructure configuration management based on IaC 2. Establish a platform framework to improve development productivity - Design CI/CD pipelines and ensure continuous improvement 3. Ensure platform stability and strengthen observability - Design, build, and operate monitoring and alerting systems based on OpenTelemetry - Perform incident response, RCA, and post-mortems 4. Handle healthcare data security and compliance - Operate infrastructure considering healthcare data security compliance 5. Develop service modules necessary for platform operation and construction - Support GPU infrastructure for AI/ML workloads

Qualifications

▶ Qualifications • 5 to 10 years of experience • Bachelor's degree or higher ▶ Required skills/knowledge • Experience designing and operating Kubernetes-based cloud-native environments (3 years or more) • Must have 5 years or more of back-end development or DevOps experience. • Experience in building and operating public cloud-based services • Experience writing Helm Charts and designing, building, and operating CI/CD pipelines • Understanding of container orchestration, networking, and storage • Operating Kafka-based message streaming environments • Experience in developing, constructing, and operating services using AI Agents

Preferred Qualifications

• Experience in building and operating OAuth 2.0 / OIDC authentication servers (Keycloak, etc.) • Experience in designing, building, and operating API Gateways (Envoy, Kong, NGINX, etc.) • Experience designing and developing REST APIs • Experience in building and operating messaging systems based on Apache Kafka • Experience in building observability systems based on OpenTelemetry (Grafana, Prometheus, Loki, etc.) • Understanding of the healthcare domain, including HL7 FHIR • Experience in designing infrastructure based on security compliance such as ISMS-P, HIPAA • Experience in serving, architecting, and operating large-scale data • Experience in building and operating MLOps pipelines • Experience in platform design, construction, and operations
