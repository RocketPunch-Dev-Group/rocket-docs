Responsibilities

Let me introduce the team you will be joining • Abley's data analysts belong to a chapter organized with squads and data analysts focused on problem-solving. • As a data analyst in a purpose-driven squad, - You will work as a team with colleagues from various disciplines such as PO / Engineers / Designers / Marketers to solve business problems based on each other's strengths and expertise. - You will lead A to Z of data analysis, from planning analysis and experiments to measuring results and participating in decision-making, with a strong sense of ownership and dedication to your work. • As a data analyst in the functional chapter, - You will share various expertise and insights and think about ways to make a greater impact on squad operations. Here are the tasks you will be doing • Proactively define the analyses and experiments needed for the success of the Abley team, explore various data, and actively propose projects / services / features that align with the team's goals. • Deliver meaningful insights gained from data analysis in structured materials to enhance the understanding of business among team members. • Support members in discussing problem-solving methods from various perspectives by analyzing problem situations from multiple angles. We would like to work with someone who • Resonates with Abley's organizational culture, willing to work GRIT as One Team and create impact. • Has experience in the entire data analysis process from data cleaning, problem definition, insight generation to action item presentation. • Can extract and process necessary data using SQL and Python. • Can communicate complex analysis results smoothly to colleagues from diverse disciplines. • Has an understanding of commerce and platform data would be a plus. • Has thoughtfully considered aspects like building metric systems, improving data accessibility, and enhancing data literacy would be even better.
