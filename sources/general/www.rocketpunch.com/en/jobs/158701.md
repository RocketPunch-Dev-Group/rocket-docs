Team Introduction

We are looking for someone who wants to solve customer problems and gain experience in operating SaaS services. CollaboSalon is a SaaS service for beauty shop operations that provides features for reservations, customer management, and marketing automation. Currently, we are operating services for real paying customers in Korea, the USA, and Brazil, catering to customers from various countries. CollaboGround aims to be a team that does not just forward customer inquiries but participates in solving customer problems and creating better services together. We create an environment where the customer's voice leads to real product improvements and where we can solve problems together with the product team and grow.

Responsibilities

\- Responding to customer inquiries and providing service usage instructions - Onboarding new customers and explaining key features - Resolving inquiries related to service operations such as reservations, customer management, and memberships - Writing and improving FAQs, guides, and response templates - Organizing frequently occurring inquiries and issues - Sharing customer feedback with the product team and participating in service improvements - Participating in function reviews from the customer's perspective before the launch of new features ※ No random sales calls or sales work involved.

Qualifications

\- More than 1 year of experience in customer interaction, customer support, or service operations - Someone who finds fulfillment in understanding and resolving customer issues - Interested in service operations and improvements rather than just responding to inquiries - Enjoys learning new services or features - Possesses smooth communication skills - Wants to collaborate with team members and grow together - Interested in gaining experience in operating SaaS services or growing in the IT service field

Preferred Qualifications

\- Experience in using or operating SaaS, platform, reservation services, CRM services - Experience using collaboration tools such as Notion, Slack, Google Workspace - Experience writing FAQs and guide documents - Experience in improving customer experience or operational improvements

Benefits & Work Environment

\[Experiences you can gain from this position\] - Real global paid SaaS service operation experience - Experience where customer feedback leads to product improvements - Experience solving problems together with the product team - Experience in operation automation and AI utilization - Opportunity to grow as a service operations expert \[Working Environment\] - 2 minutes walk from Hongdae Entrance Station, Mapo-gu, Seoul - Flexible work start time from 9 AM to 10 AM - Remote work possible (with prior notice) - Flexible annual leave - Non-inclusive wage system in operation - MacBook and dual monitors provided

Process

\- Please apply including your resume and a brief self-introduction. - Writing your application focusing on CS experience will help in the review. Document screening → Online interview → Assignment screening → Face-to-face interview → Final onboarding

Notes

📈 Growth opportunities at CollaboGround - Real customer problem-solving → Experience that connects to paid conversion and service performance - Acquiring operational, marketing, and automation capabilities in the SaaS industry and global market - Practical experience in customer-centric thinking, automation systems, and various collaborative methods
