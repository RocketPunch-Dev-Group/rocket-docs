Responsibilities

Healthcare information is complex and difficult. This is a reality faced by both patients and healthcare providers. Every day, numerous patients experience anxiety as they struggle to understand their conditions. Healthcare providers also feel regret at not being able to provide ample explanations within limited consultation time. Tesser develops AI technologies and services that allow patients and doctors to understand and effectively utilize healthcare information more easily. We alleviate the burden on patients, healthcare providers, and hospital practitioners with AI solutions optimized for healthcare. Through this, we aim to create an innovative healthcare culture where anyone can use AI and data to understand and manage their health. \[Tesser is creating the following products\] ＜Ontol (Patient App)＞ A patient app that allows users to easily understand test results by simply taking pictures, along with various services to manage health starting from chatbots and community engagement. ＜Ontol Hospital SaaS＞ An AI SaaS that offers easy-to-understand, customized explanations of test results for hospitals and health check-up centers. \[Tesser does the following\] Ontol is a service in its early stages that is rapidly growing as it is shared among patients. Tesser, 1) effectively resolves the challenges that patients face, 2) continuously discovers issues to ensure that anyone can use the service easily and conveniently, addressing them through AI technologies and services. ■ Major Responsibilities - Backend development for 'Ontol' platform, a next-generation healthcare service focused on patient data - Development of Ontol's hospital SaaS backend - Development of Node.js based backend APIs on AWS cloud for web and mobile user services ■ Requirements - Backend engineer with 3+ years of experience - Experience in utilizing AWS cloud - Understanding of RDB, SQL, and experience using ORM - Understanding and experience in setting up Docker container environments - Experience with version control and collaboration using Git ■ Preferred Qualifications - Someone who can discuss problem-solving through services, not just engineering - A person who enjoys deeply considering and executing strategies to improve service quality and team capabilities - Someone who keeps up with and wants to follow technological trends - Those with technical experiences like below - Experience with ES6 and Node.js development - Building AWS environments using VPC, ECS, and Lambda - Experience setting up CI/CD for Mono Repo using Jenkins - Primarily using Typescript - Experience with Nest.js and GraphQL ■ Benefits and Welfare • Flexible work environment • Basic coverage of four major insurances • Coffee, various teas, and snacks provided • Development environment, cloud, and GPU provided • Support for various seminars and conferences • Support for purchasing books ■ Joining Journey • Document review ＞ Online job interview ＞ Offline culture fit interview ＞ Salary negotiation ＞ Acceptance and onboarding
