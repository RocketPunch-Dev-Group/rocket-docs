Team Introduction

A-Fin Inc. is developing both external SI projects and internal solutions centered on a diverse portfolio of fintech services. We are a technology-based agency that consults and executes, spanning both digital transformation (DT) and process innovation. Most employees, including the CEO, are former developers who fully understand the importance and challenges of development work and are continuously striving to resolve them. The agency division diagnoses client systems and processes, from As-Is analysis, requirements specifications, information structure design, security diagnosis, to deriving PI promotion tasks, we are directly involved and execute every stage from planning to design to execution. Furthermore, depending on one's capabilities and willingness, we provide an environment where one can autonomously participate in various stages, including planning, design, and security, beyond technical implementation. We are major official partners of AWS and Oracle, applying various cloud technologies such as MySQL and MySQL HeatWave in practice, going beyond simple application development to handle expanded technological spectrums including cloud infrastructure composition, deployment, operational environment optimization, and data pipeline design. We also provide an environment where you can experience collaboration and technical discussions directly with the MySQL engineering team, a key technology partner of Oracle, allowing involvement in engine-level feature improvements or new feature introductions. With a relatively younger average employee age, we avoid unnecessary practices such as company dinners and aim for a flat organizational culture. Moreover, we do not believe that years of experience prove skills. While the depth of experience can make a difference, if a second-year employee shows performance at the level of a fourth-year employee, we offer treatment equivalent to that of a fourth-year or more. We believe that the essence of development comes from the ability to solve problems and flexible thinking, unfettered by tools or languages. We are looking for developers who can quickly learn the necessary tools and apply the right technology, whether in front-end, back-end, or at the intersections of both.

Responsibilities

\- Problem solving by traversing between front and backend areas - Development and enhancement of features for B2B CMS solutions - Customization and implementation of new features based on customer requirements - Establishment and operation of linked systems based on global e-commerce solutions

Qualifications

\- For front-end, experience in React.js; for back-end, experience in Node.js or Kotlin + Spring based projects (Having one of them is acceptable, and those who can flexibly expand are preferred) - Understanding of JavaScript/TypeScript - Basic CS knowledge of web development (HTTP, REST, etc.) - Excellent adaptability to new environments unfettered by technology stacks - Minimum of 1 year of experience or equivalent skills - Ability to communicate effectively with peers

Preferred Qualifications

\- Experience participating in various development projects and SI tasks - Collaboration experience with various roles such as designers/planners - Experience in both front-end and back-end or high willingness to learn - Experience using MySQL or MySQL HeatWave - Experience building/operating commerce stores based on Shopify - Major in computer science or equivalent development capabilities

Benefits & Work Environment

\- Unlimited lunch support (corporate card provided for each individual) - Free atmosphere (self-directed vacation usage, casual dress code) - Option for remote work twice a month - Support for bereavement expenses and bereavement leave - Provision of work equipment (4K dual monitor, development laptop, etc.)

Process

The hiring process proceeds in the order of 'document screening → preliminary technical tasks → job interview'.
