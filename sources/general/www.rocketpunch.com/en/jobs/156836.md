Team Introduction

Double Media is creating a world filled with unique joy by communicating in real-time through the live streaming platform "Panda TV". We are looking for talent who can enjoy innovation with an active attitude even in a rapidly changing environment. Double Media is making new connections and experiences even at this moment. We hope your passion and ideas will be a part of this journey.

Responsibilities

\- Backend development based on Go language (Experience in Node.js, Java, PHP, and Python is welcome) - API performance improvement

Qualifications

\- Applicants should have more than 3 years of backend development experience in Node.js, Java, PHP, or Python, or possess comparable skills. - Must have the willingness to challenge backend development using Go language. - Experience in projects involving database integration (MySQL, Redis, ElasticSearch, etc.). - Basic understanding of HTTP communication and experience in designing and developing RESTful APIs. - Must be proactive in discovering and solving problems, pursuing continuous improvement. - Should know the joy of collaboration and be able to communicate actively yet humbly.

Preferred Qualifications

\- Candidates should hold a bachelor's degree or higher in a relevant IT field. - Experience in API development using Go language is preferred. - Experience in building OAuth2-based authentication servers and applying them to services is a plus. - Proficient in RDB design and SQL query writing and optimization. - Familiar with collaborative work using Git. - Experience in building and optimizing AWS infrastructure and services is a plus. - Experience in handling large-scale traffic distribution is preferred.

Benefits & Work Environment

\- Long-term service rewards - Snack bar - Summer vacation allowance - Team expenses support - Book purchase support - Birthday support - Comprehensive health check-up - Holiday support - In-house cafe - Transportation fee support for long-distance residents - Support for condolence money - Moving expenses support
