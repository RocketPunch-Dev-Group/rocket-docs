Team Introduction

"No need to code everything yourself. We are looking for someone crazy about 'making' with AI." SOYOYU is a deep tech startup leading search optimization (XEO) and Edge SEO technology in the era of generative AI. We are changing "the future of search" and looking for early members to join us on this journey.

Responsibilities

\[AI Pair Programming\] Use Gemini, Claude, GPT, etc. to implement web services and features at lightning speed. \[Rapid Prototyping\] Work with the CEO to concretize ideas and create a working demo within 1-2 days to test market reactions. \[Product Fit\] Not just implementing simple features, but refining the product by considering "what value will it bring to customers." \[SOYOYU Product Development\] SEOX: Advancing AI search optimization solutions Stardustee: Adding features to personal branding AI tools Deep Tech Edge Engine: Edge computing experiments based on Cloudflare (technical challenges)

Qualifications

. AI Tool Proficiency: Must be very familiar with AI coding tools like Cursor.ai, GitHub Copilot, Replit. . Logic Over Syntax: Ability to design logical structures and give precise instructions (Prompting) to AI rather than memorizing syntax. . Problem Solver: Someone who finds solutions, saying "AI suggested this would work!" instead of "This doesn't work." . Learning Agility: A tendency to quickly learn and apply new tools and technologies. . Basic Web Knowledge: A fundamental understanding of HTTP, HTML/CSS, API is essential (even non-majors with launching experience are okay).

Preferred Qualifications

. Experience launching services from planning to development to distribution (failure experiences are also welcome). . Experience using Next.js, Python, Cloudflare Workers. . Interest and understanding of SEO, LLM, RAG technologies. . No reluctance to read and learn English technical documents.

Benefits & Work Environment

. Full support for the latest paid AI tools (ChatGPT Plus, Claude, GitHub Copilot, etc.). . Support for books and courses necessary for growth. . Autonomous working environment and flexible working hours. . Possibility of compensation discussions, such as stock options, as an early member growing with the company.

Process

. Document screening (resume or portfolio). . First interview (coffee chat format, discussion of 'Vibe Coding' experience and portfolio). . Final acceptance and negotiation of treatment.

Notes

Documents to be submitted: . Resume or portfolio (format free). . A brief description of your thoughts on "Vibe Coding, GEO/SEO," or any experience you have in creating something using AI would be great.
