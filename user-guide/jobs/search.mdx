Rocketpunch connects you with thousands of tech opportunities across Korea's startup and enterprise ecosystem. Whether you're a frontend engineer hunting for a React role at a fast-growing startup or a seasoned DevOps professional targeting large-scale infrastructure teams, the job search page gives you the tools to cut through the noise and surface the positions that actually match what you're looking for.

## Keyword Search

The search bar at the top of the [Jobs page](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/jobs) is your starting point. It searches across job titles, company names, tech skills, and even workplace perks and benefits — so you can be as broad or as specific as you need.

Type any term that describes the kind of role or environment you want, and Rocketpunch will return listings that mention it anywhere in the posting.

**Multi-keyword search with commas**

You can search for multiple terms at once by separating them with commas. Rocketpunch treats each comma-separated term as an independent keyword and returns listings that match any of them — letting you cast a wider net or combine requirements in a single query.

For example, entering:

```
Next.js, PowerPoint, lunch allowance, loan interest support, performance bonus
```

…returns listings that mention Next.js skills, require PowerPoint, or offer perks like subsidised lunches, loan interest support, or performance bonuses. This makes it easy to filter not just by technology, but by the benefits that matter most to you.

<Tip>
  Use specific skill keywords like `React`, `Kubernetes`, or `TypeScript` to find roles that explicitly list those technologies in the job description — rather than relying on broad category filters alone.
</Tip>

## Filters

Use the filter bar beneath the search field to narrow your results along four dimensions. You can apply multiple filters at the same time.

  <Card title="Seniority" icon="layer-group" href="https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/jobs">
    Narrow results to entry-level, mid-level, or senior positions. Use this filter to avoid wading through listings that don't match your years of experience.

  <Card title="Company Size" icon="building" href="https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/jobs">

  <Card title="Work Type" icon="location-dot" href="https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/jobs">
    Filter by remote, in-office, or hybrid arrangements. If location flexibility is a priority, use this filter first to exclude listings that don't meet your needs before reviewing the rest.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Sort Options

Once you have your keywords and filters in place, use the sort menu to control the order in which results appear.

| Sort Option | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Relevance** | Ranks listings by how closely they match your search keywords. Best when you have specific terms entered. |
| **Popularity** | Surfaces listings that have attracted the most candidate interest. Useful for gauging which roles are generating buzz. |
| **Latest** | Shows the most recently posted jobs first. Use this to stay on top of new openings as companies post them. |
| **By Company Size** | Orders results from largest to smallest company (or vice versa). Helpful when company scale is your primary consideration. |

<Note>
  Job listings on Rocketpunch are posted in Korean or English depending on the hiring company. If you primarily read English, look for listings that include an English-language description, or use the platform's bilingual interface to navigate postings.
</Note>
