Creating a Rocketpunch account gives you immediate access to thousands of tech job listings, company profiles, networking events, and educational programs — all for free. The sign-up process takes under two minutes and uses your email address to get you started. Once you're in, you can build your professional profile and begin connecting with Korea's tech ecosystem right away.

## Sign Up for Rocketpunch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Rocketpunch and open the login page">
    Visit [rocketpunch.com](https://www.rocketpunch.com) and click **Log In** in the top-right corner of the page. You can also go directly to [rocketpunch.com/en/login](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/login).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address and continue">
    Type your email address into the field and click **Continue**. Rocketpunch uses email-based authentication — there's no separate password to create upfront.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification message from Rocketpunch. Open the email and follow the link or enter the confirmation code to verify your address and activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your basic profile">
    Once your email is verified, you'll be prompted to add your name, headline, and a few key details. Fill these in to make your profile discoverable. You can always add more information later — see [Set Up Your Profile](/user-guide/profile-setup) for a full walkthrough.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Login Options

Rocketpunch uses **email-based login**, keeping authentication straightforward and secure. Enter your registered email address, complete the verification step, and you're in. There are no third-party OAuth logins required. When you create your account, you agree to Rocketpunch's [Terms of Service](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/privacy). These documents outline how your data is stored and used on the platform.

## Language Settings

Rocketpunch supports **11 languages**, making it accessible to international tech professionals working in or with the Korean market. The platform defaults to Korean, but you can switch to English or another supported language at any time.

To change your display language:

1. Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner.
2. Open **Settings**.
3. Under **Language**, select your preferred language from the dropdown.
4. Save your changes — the interface updates immediately.

English-language users can also access the platform directly at [rocketpunch.com/en](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en) to start in English mode.

<Tip>
  Completing your profile right after sign-up significantly increases your visibility to recruiters. Profiles with a photo, headline, at least one work experience entry, and a list of skills appear much more frequently in company and recruiter searches. See [Set Up Your Profile](/user-guide/profile-setup) to get started.
</Tip>

## Account Security

Keep your Rocketpunch account secure by using a strong, unique password for the email address associated with your account and reviewing your active sessions periodically in **Settings → Security**. Avoid sharing your login credentials, and be cautious of phishing emails that imitate Rocketpunch communications — official messages will always come from a `@rocketpunch.com` address.

If you're locked out of your account, suspect unauthorized access, or need help with account recovery, contact the support team directly at [support@rocketpunch.com](mailto:support@rocketpunch.com).

<Note>
  By completing sign-up, you agree to Rocketpunch's [Terms of Service](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.rocketpunch.com/en/privacy). You can review both documents in full before creating your account.
</Note>
