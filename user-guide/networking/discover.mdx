## Interacting with Content

You can engage with posts in your feed in several ways to save useful content and share it with your network.

**Save posts for later**

Click the bookmark icon on any post to add it to your saved items. Access your saved posts at any time from your profile to revisit articles, announcements, or discussions you want to reference.

**Repost to your network**

Use the repost button to share a post with your own followers on Rocketpunch. Reposting is a quick way to amplify content you find valuable and keep your network informed.

**Share externally**

Use the share option to copy a link or send a post outside of Rocketpunch — via messaging apps, email, or social media. Sharing helps you bring relevant content to colleagues who may not yet be on the platform.

## Personalizing Your Feed

The more you engage with Discover, the more relevant your feed becomes. You can actively shape what you see by following topics and people.

**Follow topics and interests**

Select topics that align with your professional interests — such as artificial intelligence, fintech, design, or developer tools. Rocketpunch uses your topic preferences to surface content that matches your focus areas.

**Follow companies and people**

Visit any company profile or user profile and click **Follow** to add their posts to your feed. Following keeps you in the loop on updates from organizations you admire and professionals whose work you respect.

**Unfollow to refine your feed**

If a source no longer feels relevant, unfollow it at any time from the profile page or your following list. Keeping your follow list intentional ensures your feed stays focused and useful.

<Tip>
  Follow companies you're interested in working for to see their latest updates, culture posts, and job openings directly in your Discover feed — before you even start a formal job search.
</Tip>
