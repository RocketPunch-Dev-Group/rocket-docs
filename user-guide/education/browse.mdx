## Search and Filters

Rocketpunch gives you several tools to narrow thousands of listings down to exactly the program you need.

### Keyword Search

Type any course name, institution name, or curriculum term into the search bar to find matching programs. To search for multiple topics at once, separate your keywords with commas.

**Example:** `DBA, React, HRD Korea, Gangseo-gu Busan`

This returns programs that match any of the supplied terms, letting you cast a wide net or triangulate on a very specific niche in a single query.

### Filter Options

Use the filter panel alongside search results to refine by:

- **Category** — Limit results to one of the featured topic areas listed above.
- **Eligibility** — Filter by who can enroll, such as employed workers, job seekers, or students, which is especially useful for government-subsidised programs.
- **Training Type** — Choose between bootcamp-style intensives, structured academy courses, one-on-one or group mentoring, and self-paced online modules.
- **Online / Offline** — Show only remote-friendly programs, only in-person programs, or both.

### Sort Order

Toggle between two sort options at the top of the results list:

- **Recommended** — Surfaces programs that Rocketpunch editors have highlighted based on quality and relevance.
- **Closing Soon** — Ranks programs with the nearest application deadline first, so you never miss a registration window.

## Types of Programs

Understanding the format of a program before you apply saves time and helps you set realistic expectations.

**Bootcamps** are intensive, time-boxed training experiences — typically four to twelve weeks — focused on getting you job-ready as quickly as possible. They usually include project work, code reviews, and career coaching.

**Academy Programs** are structured multi-month curricula from established education companies. They follow a more traditional class format with scheduled lectures, assignments, and assessments.

**Mentoring Programs** pair you with an experienced practitioner in your field. Sessions can be one-on-one or small-group and are suited to professionals who already have a baseline and want targeted guidance from someone working in the industry.

**Online Courses** are self-paced or cohort-based video and text courses you can complete from anywhere. They range from short topic primers to comprehensive certificate programs.
